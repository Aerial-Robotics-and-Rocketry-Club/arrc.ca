MESSAGE FROM OUR President

The Aerial Robotics & Rocketry Club, founded in 2015 by Blago and Ilija Hristovski at UBC's Okanagan campus, has been a pioneer in creating aerospace opportunities for students. Initially, options for pursuing aerospace engineering were limited, but the club has transformed this landscape by providing a dynamic environment for students to explore their passions. Thanks to the club and its supporters, "aerospace" is now a prominent topic at UBCâ€™s Okanagan campus.

For the 2023/2024 season, we aim to advance our divisions and excel in upcoming competitions.

The club also hosts a variety of events including industry nights, learning workshops, and social events. There is always a need for student volunteers with a passion for aerospace and a strong work ethic at Aerial Robotics and Rocketry Club. Competition Divisions

Our Competition Division consists of two subdivisions.
Each is a team of dedicated members, vying for their next challenge. With upcoming events, our teams are regularly working on upgrades to our vehicle fleet.

Our plans for the upcoming year include developing new payloads, general upgrades and continually preparing to compete in future competitions!
Message us for details!

Rocketry

Rocketry has quickly become a new and engaging division within the UBCO Aerospace Club. We are currently working on a propulsion system for sounding rockets, with the purpose of atmospheric science research.

The Rocketry Division is highly encouraged for first years and is always welcoming new members.

Previous projects include the 'SLR', landing an ~8ft rocket under its own propulsion which required a number of members each with diverse backgrounds, no matter the experience.

We look forward to advertising our upcoming projects and encourage engagement of all backgrounds.

We also love to test our regular 7ft Rocket for practice, and for new members to get involved right away! Come work with us, and explore your passion for Rocketry.
Message us for information.

Director of Rocketry: Benjamin Van Hattem
aerialroboticsrocketry@gmail.com

Rotary UAV Divisions

Our rotary division is our longest ongoing division. With previous projects such as the, S500, Jellyfish and Oreo - there is really a spot for everyone to try something new.

General goals for the Rotary Division include long range testing, surveying, as well as working with our sponsors for an array of different goals. This includes be able to operate in all weather conditions.

Current projects ongoing: Racing FPV helicopters on a 5" or 7" frame
